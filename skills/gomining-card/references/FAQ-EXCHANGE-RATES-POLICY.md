# Exchange Rates and Fees Policy

## Are There Fixed Fees for Asset Conversion?

No. GoMining does not charge a separate fixed fee for converting assets during wallet–card transfers or card transactions.

The final amount depends on:
- **Market conditions** at the time the transaction is processed.
- **Payment and exchange infrastructure** involved in the conversion.
- An **operational spread** is included in the rate — this is not fixed and varies based on asset liquidity and processing time.

GoMining does not publish fixed percentages (e.g., "1% for BTC") because the actual rate differs per transaction. The full conversion result is shown at the time of the transaction.

## Top-Up Restrictions

The GoMining Card can only be topped up from your **GoMining internal wallet**. Direct top-ups via Revolut, traditional bank transfers, or other external platforms are not supported.

## Transaction Visibility: Google Pay vs App

If a transaction shows in Google Pay but not in the GoMining app:

- **Google Pay** displays transactions immediately upon authorization.
- The **GoMining app** displays them only after **final settlement**, which can take a few business days.

Steps:
1. Wait **1–2 additional business days**.
2. If it still doesn't appear, update the app and clear the cache.
3. If the issue persists after these steps, contact support.

## Does VIP or Cashback Change the Exchange Rate?

No. Cashback and VIP levels do **not** change the base exchange rate or conversion mechanism.

- These programs increase the overall value of using the card.
- Higher VIP tiers provide higher cashback percentages — acting as a return on operations — but the underlying exchange rate remains market-based.

## Will Rates Improve in the Future?

GoMining is continuously working to optimize transaction processing. However, specific guaranteed spread levels or fee reductions cannot be predicted or promised in advance.
