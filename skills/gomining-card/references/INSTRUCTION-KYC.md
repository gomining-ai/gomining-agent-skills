# Pass Identity Verification (KYC)

### Overview

KYC is an identity verification process that protects your account and transactions from fraud and ensures regulatory compliance.

GoMining has two KYC levels:

#### KYC Level 1

* Checks only your identity via document submission and selfie
* You can use: Valid national passport, ID, Residence card, Driver's license
* Required to: withdraw GOMINING token, unlock more BTC withdrawal options, mint/withdraw/sell miners, or access all GoMining Launchpad projects.
* Available in all countries except prohibited jurisdictions like North Korea, Myanmar, Iran, Afghanistan, Syrian Arab Republic, Yemen, and Cuba.

#### KYC Level 2

* Checks your identity and collects additional compliance details (email, phone number, tax information, proof of address, and a financial questionnaire).
* Required to issue a GoMining Visa card.

### How to Pass KYC Level 1

1. Go to **Profile** → **Security**
2. Select the **KYC** option
3. Click **Pass KYC**
4. Prepare your documents
5. Click **Start verification**
6. Select the country that issued your identity document and the type of document
7. Upload a live photo of your identity document. It should be bright and clear, with all corners of the document visible.
8. Take a selfie turning your head in a circle
9. Check the provided information.

The document should be reviewed within 3-5 minutes, but in some cases can take up to 48 hours. The verification status will be updated automatically, and we'll notify you via email.

### How to Pass KYC Level 2

KYC Level 2 is available only if you're eligible.

1. Go to **Profile** → **Security**
2. Select the **KYC** option
3. Under the **Level 2** section, click the **Pass KYC** button
4. Click the **Start verification** button
5. Enter your email address to receive and submit a unique verification code
6. Select your country of tax residence
7. Choose your document type (requirements may vary by country). A driver's license is not accepted as a valid proof of identity document.
8. Provide details regarding your employment status, source of funds, and annual income
9. Submit your mobile number to receive and confirm a security code via SMS
10. Take a selfie turning your head in a circle
11. Provide proof of the address

Your proof of address must be issued within the last 3 months and clearly show your full name and your residential address.

**Accepted Proof of Address (POA) documents:**

* Government organization: statements, voter registration, tax bills
* Utility provider: utility bills, telecom bills
* Bank: bank statements, bank letters
* Mobile operator: statements and bills for mobile communications
* Other: documents from employer, rent bills, lease agreements

The document should be reviewed within 3-5 minutes, but in some cases can take up to 48 hours.

If you have problems passing KYC verification, write us at support@gomining.com.
