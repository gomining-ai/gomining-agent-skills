# Cards Instructions

This section contains step-by-step instructions for managing your GoMining Card — from issuing it for the first time to handling day-to-day tasks like topping up, changing settings, or blocking the card.

---

### In this section

#### Get Your Virtual Card

How to issue your GoMining Card.

#### View Your Card Details

How to reveal your full card number, expiry date, and CVV directly in the app.

#### Top-Up and Withdrawal

How to transfer funds from your GoMining wallet to your card balance.

#### Choose the Crypto Asset for Payments

How to select which asset (BTC, GOMINING, USDT, or USDC) the card uses for spending.

#### Set a New PIN Code

How to request a new auto-generated PIN, which will be delivered to you via SMS.

#### Block or Unblock Your Card

How to temporarily block your card if needed and unblock it without changing your card number.

#### Change Email or Phone Number

Contact details used for card operations (SMS PIN, 3DS authentication).
