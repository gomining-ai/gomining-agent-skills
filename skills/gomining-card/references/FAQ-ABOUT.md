# About the GoMining Card

### Overview

**GoMining Cards** let you spend your crypto anywhere Visa is accepted. When you make a purchase, your selected crypto asset is instantly converted into the purchase currency (EUR, USD, or any other currency), and the payment is processed as a regular Visa transaction.

You don't need to top up the card externally: all payments are made directly from your GoMining card wallet balance.

Only users who have completed both **KYC Level 1** and **Level 2** can issue and use GoMining Cards.

### What is the GoMining Card?

It's a virtual (and later, physical) Visa debit card linked to your GoMining crypto card wallet balance.

When you pay with the card, your selected crypto asset is converted to fiat at the moment of the transaction.

### What cryptocurrencies can I spend?

You can choose which asset your GoMining Card uses for payments:

* **GOMINING**
* **BTC**
* **USDT**
* **USDC**

Conversion occurs instantly for each transaction at the current exchange rate.

### What card options does GoMining offer?

GoMining virtual debit cards:

* Instantly issued once your application is approved
* Ready for online payments immediately
* Compatible with Apple Pay and Google Wallet for contactless NFC payments
* Secure with a PIN and static 3DS password for online transactions

### Am I eligible?

**GoMining Cards** are available to users who complete **KYC Level 1 and 2**.

Eligibility depends on your **country of residence**, determined by the **proof of address (POA)** submitted during verification.

#### If you live in Europe

You must submit:

* A valid **national passport** or **national ID card**
* A valid **proof of address (POA)**, no older than 3 months

Eligible European countries include: Albania, Andorra, Austria, Belgium, Bosnia and Herzegovina, Bulgaria, Croatia, Cyprus, Czechia, Denmark, Estonia, Finland, France, Germany, Greece, Hungary, Iceland, Ireland, Italy, Latvia, Liechtenstein, Lithuania, Luxembourg, Malta, Monaco, Montenegro, Netherlands, Norway, Poland, Portugal, Romania, San Marino, Serbia, Slovakia, Slovenia, Spain, Sweden, Switzerland, Ukraine, Vatican.

A driver's license and residence permit are not accepted as primary proof of identity. However, non-European citizens residing in Europe may be required to provide a residence permit to confirm their residency in Europe.

#### If you live in the rest of the world

Only residents of non-prohibited jurisdictions are eligible to apply for the card.

To apply, you must submit:

* A valid **national passport** or **national ID card** (where supported)
* A valid **proof of address (POA)** from a non-prohibited jurisdiction, no older than 3 months

Proof of identity and proof of address can be from different countries.

Passport holders of Belarus and Russia may apply only if they hold a valid residence permit issued by an EEA+ country (proof required).

#### Prohibited Jurisdictions

GoMining does not allow individuals residing in prohibited jurisdictions to open cards. Prohibited jurisdictions include: Afghanistan, Algeria, Angola, Antarctica, Bangladesh, Belarus, Benin, Bolivia, Bouvet Island, British Indian Ocean Territory, British Virgin Islands, Burkina Faso, Burundi, Cameroon, Canada, Central African Republic, Chad, Chile, China, Christmas Island, Comoros, Congo, Côte d'Ivoire, Cuba, Democratic Republic of the Congo, Djibouti, Egypt, Eritrea, Ethiopia, French Southern Territories, Gambia, Guinea, Guinea-Bissau, Haiti, Heard Island and McDonald Islands, Hong Kong, India, Indonesia, Iran, Iraq, Israel, Kenya, Kosovo, Laos, Lebanon, Lesotho, Liberia, Libya, Macao, Mali, Mauritania, Mozambique, Myanmar, Namibia, Nepal, Nicaragua, Niger, Norfolk Island, North Korea, Pakistan, Palestine, Pitcairn, Russia, Sierra Leone, Somalia, South Georgia and the South Sandwich Islands, South Sudan, Sudan, Syrian Arab Republic, Taiwan, Togo, Trinidad and Tobago, Tunisia, Turkey, US Minor Outlying Islands, Vanuatu, Venezuela, Vietnam, Western Sahara, Yemen, Zimbabwe.

### How much balance is required to create a card?

To create your first card, you need at least **$100 in your GoMining wallet**, calculated across all assets.

After the first card is issued, additional cards can be created without any balance requirement.

### How many virtual cards can I have?

You can have **up to 3 virtual GoMining Cards** active at once.

### Is there a cost to create a card?

No. All GoMining Cards are issued free of charge.
