# GoMining Card — Where and How to Use

## Where the Card Works

The GoMining Card works wherever **Visa cards** are accepted:

- **Physical stores:** retail locations, supermarkets, and other outlets that accept Visa.
- **Online stores:** one-time purchases on e-commerce sites.
- **ATMs:** cash withdrawals from ATMs that accept Visa (see below).

## Unsupported Functions

| Feature | Supported? |
|---------|-----------|
| Recurring/subscription payments (streaming, auto-renewals) | No |
| Bank transfers via IBAN | No |
| External deposits from other cards or bank accounts | No |
| Card-to-card transfers (top-up via card number) | No |
| Balance inquiries at ATMs | No — ATMs always show €0; check balance in the GoMining app |

## ATM Cash Withdrawal

You can withdraw cash from ATMs using your **virtual card** via Apple Pay or Google Wallet:

1. Link your card to Google Pay or Apple Pay.
2. Find an ATM with contactless (NFC) support.
3. Tap your phone against the NFC reader.
4. Enter your PIN.
5. Request the withdrawal.

ATM limits: up to €1,000/day and €5,000/month.

## Card Currency Conversion

When you make a purchase, the selected crypto is instantly converted into:
- **EUR** for EEA+ country users
- **USD** for other eligible countries

## Card Order Status: Processing

Once payment is made, the card provider may keep the transaction in **Processing** status for up to **30 days**. This is standard banking procedure.

- No action is required during this 30-day window.
- If the status remains unchanged after **31 days**, contact support.

## Card Issuance Timing

- Standard card issuance: up to **30 minutes**.
- In some cases, up to **24 hours** due to network or verification delays.

## Missing Email Verification Code

If you are not receiving the code to issue your card:

1. Check your **Spam/Junk** folder.
2. If not found, try using a **different email address**.
