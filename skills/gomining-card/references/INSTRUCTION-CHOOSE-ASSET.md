# Choose the Crypto Asset for Payments

The selected asset applies to all your GoMining cards. It is not possible to set a separate spending asset for each card.

1. Open the Assets → Cards section.
2. Scroll to the **Settings** section.
3. Choose the **Paying with** option.
4. Select the asset you want to use for card payments:
   * BTC
   * GOMINING
   * USDT
   * USDC
5. Click the **Save** button.

All future transactions across your cards will be charged from the selected asset.
