# Troubleshooting

### I'm not receiving the SMS activation code for my card

1. Check your **Spam or Blocked Messages** folder.
2. Do not resend repeatedly — each resend invalidates the previous code and resets the timer. Wait for the timer to expire before requesting a new code.
3. Contact your mobile operator — some carriers block automated short-code messages.
4. Wait a few hours and try again.

---

### A transaction appears in Google Pay but not in my GoMining app

Google Pay displays transactions immediately upon authorization. The GoMining app only shows them after final settlement, which can take **a few business days**.

1. Wait **1–2 additional business days**.
2. If it still doesn't appear, update the app and clear the cache.
3. If the issue persists, contact support.

---

### Why does the ATM show a €0 balance for my GoMining Card?

ATMs always show **€0** because GoMining Cards do not support balance inquiries via ATM systems.

Your actual card balance is managed inside the GoMining ecosystem and depends on:

* The amount of crypto you have allocated for card spending
* The internal conversion to EUR during transactions

Since this balance is not stored on a traditional bank account, ATMs cannot access or display it.

To check your real balance, please use the **GoMining app**.

### Why was my card issuance declined?

The card provider may decline your card request if it does not pass their internal checks and requirements.

If this happens, the card cannot be reissued for the same account.

### My balance went negative after a purchase. Why?

This happens because of exchange rate fluctuations between the time your payment is authorized (the hold) and when it is finalized (settlement). If the exchange rate changes, the final charge may exceed the initial amount, resulting in a negative balance.

To continue using your card, just add more funds to your account.
