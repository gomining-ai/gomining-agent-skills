# GoMining Academy

### What is GoMining Academy?

GoMining Academy is an educational platform designed to help you understand Bitcoin, blockchain, crypto mining, and the GoMining ecosystem. Whether you're a beginner or looking to deepen your knowledge, our step-by-step lessons, tutorials, articles, and interactive content make it easy to learn at your own pace.

---

### Who is GoMining Academy for?

GoMining Academy is ideal for:

* Crypto beginners curious about Bitcoin and mining
* GoMining token holders who want to learn how to maximise utility
* Users exploring how blockchain, Web3, and cryptocurrencies work
* Anyone interested in understanding the future of digital assets

---

### Is GoMining Academy free?

Yes, all courses and materials in GoMining Academy are completely free. Our mission is to make mining and crypto education accessible to everyone.

---

### What topics are covered in the Academy?

The Academy covers a wide range of topics, including:

* Bitcoin fundamentals and history
* How Bitcoin mining works
* GoMining and its ecosystem
* Cryptocurrencies fundamentals
* Wallet setup, token management, and staking
* How to avoid scams
* Updates and new features in the GoMining ecosystem

---

### How do I start learning?

Simply visit academy.gomining.com, sign up or log in, and choose a course. Lessons are presented in a beginner-friendly format with visuals, quizzes, and real-world examples.

---

### Do I need to own GoMining tokens to use the Academy?

No, you don't need to own GoMining tokens to access the educational materials. However, some materials are designed to help GOMINING token holders understand how to use the token effectively.

---

### Will I get a certificate after completing the courses?

Yes! When you complete a course, you receive a digital certificate — it serves as a personal achievement within the GoMining ecosystem and may be used to showcase your learning progress. You can even download it and share it with your friends.

---

### Are new learning materials added regularly?

Yes! We continuously update the GoMining Academy with new courses, articles, and guides to reflect the latest developments in the GoMining ecosystem and the broader crypto world.

---

### Is the GoMining Academy a licensed educational institution?

No. The GoMining Academy is not a licensed educational institution and does not issue any formal academic degrees or government-recognized certifications. However, once you complete a course, you receive a digital certificate — it serves as a personal achievement within the GoMining ecosystem and may be used to showcase your learning progress.

While the content is created by the GoMining team in collaboration with industry experts and thoroughly reviewed for accuracy, it is provided for informational and educational purposes only. It should not be considered professional, financial, legal, or investment advice.
