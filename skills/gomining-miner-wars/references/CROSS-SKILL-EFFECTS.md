# Cross-skill Effects on Miner Wars

How other GoMining products and statuses interact with Miner Wars. Use this when answering questions like "do my VIP / Platinum+ / referral / avatar / bonus miner perks affect Miner Wars?"

---

## VIP Program

| VIP Tier | Effect in Miner Wars |
|----------|----------------------|
| **Bronze II – Elite** | Maintenance discount in Miner Wars Mode (0.3% → 6%) |
| **Gold I+** | Unlocks **Clan creation** (up to 3 Clans/year) |
| **Gold I+ (as Clan Owner)** | Earns **5% GOMINING royalties** from members' Clan activity |
| **Owner drops below Gold I** | >24h before Cycle end: keep ownership through Cycle, restore status or lose ownership; <24h: keep through *next* Cycle |

Threshold reminder: Gold I = 100+ TH **or** 1000+ veGOMINING votes.

---

## Platinum+ Subscription

Active Platinum+ gives:

| Benefit | Effect in Miner Wars |
|---------|----------------------|
| **15% spell discount** | Applies to every spell purchase, including those cast by **Spell Bot** |
| Platinum I privileges | Includes Miner Wars-applicable maintenance discount |

**Important stacking rule:** the 15% Platinum+ spell discount **does not stack** with GoMiners Avatar discounts — the **highest** one applies by default.

---

## GOMINING Token

The Miner Wars game is denominated in GMT for spend, and pays GMT for personal-battle wins:

| Use | How |
|-----|-----|
| **Spell currency** | Every spell and power-up costs GOMINING |
| **Spell Bot budget** | Separate GMT budget the bot draws from |
| **Maintenance discount (1–20%)** | Pay miner maintenance in GMT for the largest discount tier |
| **Personal rewards** | Won via personal battles, paid in GMT |

---

## veGOMINING / Tokenomics

| Mechanism | Effect on Miner Wars |
|-----------|----------------------|
| **Weekly emissions vote** | veGOMINING holders vote how the 10% weekly emission share is allocated — one option is the **Miner Wars prize fund** (additional GMT for Odyssey League winners) |
| **GMT spent on spells** | 97.5% goes into the personal-battle prize pool (deflationary effect on circulating spend) |
| **veGOMINING as Gold I path** | 1000+ votes counts toward Gold I, unlocking Clan creation without needing 100 TH |

---

## Bonus Miner (from Promos)

**Prerequisite:** Bonus Miner has effect in Miner Wars **only if you already own at least one Digital Miner.** It is not a way to play without owning a miner.

| Scenario | Behavior |
|----------|----------|
| User owns **no** Digital Miner | Bonus Miner does **not** enable Miner Wars play — it has no in-game effect on its own |
| User **owns** a Digital Miner | Bonus Miner power **adds** to your stats in Miner Wars |
| Clan reward calculation | Bonus Miner power counts **only for rounds when it was active** |
| Clan entry requirements | Bonus Miner power is **excluded** from minimum TH/GMT checks |
| Maintenance & discounts | Once you own a Digital Miner, Bonus Miner is included in all fee/discount math (VIP, Service Button apply); Auto-reinvest in TH cannot be enabled for it |
| Reward withdrawal | Possible only after buying a miner directly from GoMining **or** opening a GoMining Card; unclaimed rewards >90 days old are auto-deleted if no qualifying purchase exists |

## Referral Program (Ambassador)

| Source | Reward |
|--------|--------|
| Referrals' GOMINING spent on Miner Wars boosts | **2.5%** to the Ambassador (calculated weekly) |
| Referrals in Miner Wars Mode | Electricity rewards calculated **weekly** at Cycle end (not daily); based on weighted-average EE |

---

## Avatars (GoMiners / Luminars)

| Avatar Type | Effect |
|-------------|--------|
| **GoMiners Avatars** | Discount on spell purchases (game-specific; does **not stack** with Platinum+ — highest applies) |
| **Luminars** | VIP level boost — indirectly affects Miner Wars discounts and Clan-creation eligibility |

## Quick Decision Matrix

> "I want maximum Miner Wars value — which products matter most?"

1. **Get to Gold I VIP** — unlocks Clan creation + royalties, highest single Miner-Wars-specific unlock
2. **Subscribe to Platinum+** if you spend on spells — 15% off every cast (and Spell Bot)
3. **Pay maintenance in GMT** — up to 20% discount, biggest maintenance lever
4. **Reach Elite VIP** for the 6% maintenance discount on top of GMT payment
5. **Lock GMT into veGOMINING** — votes can direct emissions to the Miner Wars prize fund + counts toward Gold I
