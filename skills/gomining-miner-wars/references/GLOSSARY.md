# Miner Wars Glossary

Alphabetical reference for Miner Wars terminology.

---

## A

### Ambassador
A user enrolled in the GoMining referral program who earns rewards from invited users. In Miner Wars Mode, ambassador rewards from a referral are calculated **weekly** at Cycle end (not daily), based on the referral's weighted average energy efficiency, plus 2.5% of the GMT the referral spends on boosts.

---

## B

### Base EE
The constant Energy Efficiency value used as a reference in the PPS formula. Currently **28 W/TH** (subject to change).

### Block (Bitcoin)
A confirmed Bitcoin network block. Each block confirmation **ends a round** in Miner Wars.

### Bonus Miner
A free promotional in-app miner whose power is **added on top of** owned Digital Miners' stats in Miner Wars. **Cannot be used as a standalone way to play** — you must own at least one Digital Miner for Bonus Miner power to count. Excluded from Clan entry requirements (TH/GMT minimums). See [gomining-promos](../../gomining-promos/) for full Bonus Miner mechanics (tasks, upgrades, withdrawal rules).

---

## C

### Clan
The basic game unit. Up to **1000 members** combine their TH and average EE. Joining a Clan is required to play.

### Clan Cycling
The prohibited practice of moving between Clans to manipulate league placement or monopolize promotion slots. Triggers suspension or ban.

### Clan Owner
Creator of a Clan. Requires **Gold I+** VIP. Earns **5% royalty** on GOMINING tokens won by Clan members. Can invite, approve, reject, or remove members.

### Clan Power-up
Spell that boosts every Clan member's base PPS for the current round; effect grows with each member activation (see growth table in [SPELLS.md](SPELLS.md)).

### Clan Score
Sum of all members' personal scores in a round.

### Copy Boost
Spell that copies a percentage of the **next boost cast** in the same round (does not generate points by itself). Three tiers: Copy, Super Copy (×10), Ultra Copy (×100). Must be cast — or ordered in Spell Bot — *before* the target boost.

### Cycle
A 7-day reward period running **Tuesday 00:00 UTC** to the next Tuesday 00:00 UTC. Rewards are calculated and paid at Cycle end.

---

## D

### Dune
Entry-level League. Dynamic Clan slots. Multiplier range **x1 – x32**. Reshuffled each Cycle for fairness.

---

## E

### Eclipse
Mid-tier League. 50 Clan slots. Multiplier range **x1 – x128**.

### Echo Boost
Spell that grants points every **2 minutes**, with the amount increasing over time. Best on long rounds.

### Energy Efficiency (EE)
Power consumption in **W/TH**. Lower is better — affects both passive maintenance fees and PPS in Miner Wars.

---

## F

### Focus Boost
Spell granting a large number of points after a 3-second delay, **valid for 5 minutes**. If the round ends within that window, points are credited.

---

## G

### GMT / GOMINING
The native utility token. In Miner Wars: spell currency, Spell Bot budget, maintenance discount (up to 20%), and personal-battle reward currency.

### Gold I VIP
Tier required to **create a Clan**. Reached with 100+ TH or 1000+ veGOMINING votes.

---

## H

### Horizon
League between Dune and Eclipse. 50 Clan slots. Multiplier range **x1 – x64**.

---

## I

### Instant Boost
Spell that adds points instantly (with a 3-second delay). Three tiers: Instant, Super Instant (×10), Ultra Instant (×100).

---

## L

### League
Competitive group of Clans. Four leagues: **Odyssey**, **Eclipse**, **Horizon**, **Dune**. Higher leagues unlock higher max multipliers.

### League Hopping
Prohibited practice of moving between leagues to manipulate placement or bypass difficulty scaling. Triggers suspension or ban.

---

## M

### Maintenance Discount (in Miner Wars)
Reductions in daily maintenance cost while in Miner Wars Mode:
- Miner Service spell — up to 3%
- VIP tier — 0.3% (Bronze II) to 6% (Elite)
- GMT payment — 1% to 20% (largest)

### Miner Service
Spell that grants instant points (PPS × 1000) and contributes to a cumulative maintenance discount (+0.3%/day, capped at 3% after 10 consecutive days).

### Mining Mode
The alternative earning mode for Digital Miners — passive, daily BTC rewards, no gameplay. See [MODE-COMPARISON.md](MODE-COMPARISON.md).

### Multiplier (Round Multiplier)
Weight factor (x1 – x256) assigned to a round based on how many Bitcoin blocks are mined simultaneously. Determines how heavily that round counts toward Cycle results.

---

## O

### Odyssey
Top League. 50 Clan slots. Multiplier range **x1 – x256**. Receives additional GOMINING from weekly emissions (voted via veGOMINING).

---

## P

### Platinum+
Subscription tier giving **15% spell discount** (including Spell Bot casts), 2.1% maintenance discount, and Platinum I privileges. Spell discount **does not stack** with GoMiners Avatar discount — highest applies.

### Points-per-second (PPS)
Rate of score growth per second. Formula: `PPS = user TH × (base EE / user EE)`.

### Power Boost
Spell adding extra points **every second** until the round ends. Three tiers: Power, Super Power (×10), Ultra Power (×100). Most effective when activated early.

### Power-up
A category of boost that multiplies base PPS for the round. Includes the Power-up (×100, once per round, scales in cost with PPS) and Clan Power-up.

### Promotion / Demotion
5 Clans move up or down between Odyssey, Eclipse, and Horizon each Cycle, ranked by blocks mined and TH as tiebreaker.

---

## R

### Round
Core gameplay unit. Starts and ends when a new Bitcoin block is confirmed. ~120–150 rounds per day. A new round begins immediately after the previous one ends.

---

## S

### Spell
Paid in-game effect, denominated in GMT. Categories: Instant, Power, Echo, Focus Boosts, plus Miner Service.

### Spell Bot
Official GoMining automation that casts spells based on round multiplier, drawing from its own GMT budget. Safe to use — won't trigger bans. Honors Platinum+ 15% discount.

---

## T

### TH (Terahash / Terahashes per second)
Unit of mining power. Total TH directly affects PPS.

---

## U

### Ultra Boost
The 100× tier of any boost family (Ultra Instant, Ultra Power, Ultra Echo, Ultra Focus).

---

## V

### veGOMINING
Vote-escrowed GOMINING. Holders vote on weekly emission allocation — one option is the Miner Wars prize fund (extra GMT for Odyssey winners). 1000+ votes also qualifies for Gold I VIP and Clan creation.
