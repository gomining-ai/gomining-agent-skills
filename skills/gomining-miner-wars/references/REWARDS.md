# Miner Wars Rewards

Miner Wars rewards are distributed **weekly**. Each Cycle runs from Tuesday 00:00 UTC to the next Tuesday 00:00 UTC.

Two types of rewards are distributed:

| Reward | Earned in | Who gets it |
|--------|-----------|-------------|
| **BTC** | Clan competition | All Clans that won at least 1 block during the Cycle |
| **GOMINING** | Personal battles | Personal battle winners across all leagues |

---

## BTC Rewards (Clan Competition)

### Prize fund formation

The BTC prize fund is built from the **pool rewards that all league players would have earned in Mining Mode**. In other words, the BTC the same group of miners would have produced as passive Mining-Mode rewards becomes the competitive prize pool instead.

### Distribution

* Distributed among all Clans that won **at least one block** during the Cycle
* Within a Clan, BTC is split among members **proportional to their individual mining power**
* Higher leagues capture larger shares of the fund (driven by their higher max multipliers)

---

## GOMINING Rewards (Personal Battles)

The GOMINING prize fund accumulates weekly and is distributed among personal battle winners across all leagues.

**The fund is formed from three sources:**

| Source | Share |
|--------|-------|
| GOMINING spent on boosts and spells | **97.5%** routed into the prize pool |
| Weekly emissions (Odyssey League winners) | Additional GOMINING, voted via veGOMINING governance |
| Marketing additions | Periodic top-ups from GoMining campaigns |

> The fact that 97.5% of all spell spending goes back into the prize pool means GOMINING rewards are largely a **redistribution** mechanism among active players — competitive spending raises the pool for everyone.

## Maintenance Discounts in Miner Wars

Players in Miner Wars Mode can still reduce maintenance costs:

| Discount | Range | How to obtain |
|----------|-------|---------------|
| **Miner Service** spell button | up to **3%** | Use the Miner Service spell daily for 10 consecutive days (+0.3%/day cumulative) |
| **VIP discount** | **0.3% – 6%** | Bronze II (0.3%) through Elite (6%) |
| **GOMINING token payment** | **1% – 20%** | Pay maintenance fees in GMT — largest single discount |

These stack with each other (subject to product rules — see [Cross-skill Effects](CROSS-SKILL-EFFECTS.md)).

---

## How Rewards Are Paid Out

* Calculated automatically at Cycle end (Tuesday 00:00 UTC)
* BTC accumulates in the GoMining virtual wallet
* GOMINING rewards land in the virtual wallet, withdrawable via BEP-20, ERC-20, or TON (up to 30,000 GMT/day)
* Withdrawal mechanics for both currencies follow standard GoMining wallet rules

---

## Referral Reward Timing in Miner Wars

For referred players in **Miner Wars Mode**, Ambassador rewards are calculated **weekly** (at Cycle end), not daily as in Mining Mode. Electricity rewards for those referrals are based on the **weighted average Energy Efficiency** of their miners.

Ambassadors also earn **2.5% of tokens their referrals spend on Miner Wars boosts** (calculated weekly).
