# Clans

## What is a Clan?

A Clan is a basic game unit in Miner Wars. It consists of players who own digital miners and combines their stats — total hash power and average energy efficiency. Clan capacity is limited to **1000 members**.

You must join a Clan to participate in the game.

---

## How to Choose a Clan

To join an existing Clan, review the list of available teams and choose one.

**When choosing a Clan, consider the following:**

* **Points-per-second rate (PPS)** is the key factor in determining victory. It shows how fast the Clan earns points.
* **League** determines the Clan's possible reward multipliers. Higher leagues offer larger BTC payouts.
* **The number of participants** indicates how many players are in the Clan.
* **Total rewards** reflect the Clan's success in past battles.

BTC rewards for winning are distributed among Clan members based on each member's personal miner stats.

---

## Can I Join Any Clan?

Clans can be **moderated** or **public**.

* **Public Clan** — you become a member automatically after applying
* **Moderated Clan** — you'll need to wait for the Clan Owner's approval; your request may be declined

### Entry requirements

Clan owners can set minimum entry requirements for **mining power (TH)** and **GOMINING balance**. Bonus miner power is **excluded** from entry requirements.

---

## How to Become a Clan Owner

Clans can be created only by users with **Gold I+** VIP status (100+ TH or 1000+ votes). You can create up to **3 Clans per year**.

**As a Clan Owner, you can:**

* Invite players, accept or reject membership requests, and remove members
* Earn a **5% commission** of the GOMINING tokens won by Clan members
* Choose to play solo — you are not required to invite anyone

If the Clan Owner leaves the Clan, ownership transfers to the member with the highest TH amount.

---

## What happens if the Clan Owner's VIP status drops?

* **More than 24 hours before the end of the cycle:** Ownership remains until the end of the cycle. The owner must restore Gold I status by then — otherwise, ownership transfers to the member with the highest TH on the following Tuesday.
* **Less than 24 hours before the end of the cycle:** The owner has until the end of the next cycle.

---

## Clan Logo Requirements

When creating or uploading a Clan logo, follow these rules:

* Use JPG or PNG format, up to **2 MB**
* The logo must be original and created specifically for your Clan
* Don't include trademarks, company names, or third-party projects
* Keep it respectful: avoid references to religion, nationality, or culture
* No offensive, obscene, or aggressive content

---

## Clan Exclusion

If you are not the Clan Owner, your membership can be revoked without explanation. If this happens, your mining farm switches to **Mining Mode** automatically.
