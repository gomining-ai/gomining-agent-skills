# Environmental Friendliness

### How eco-friendly is your mining method?

Part of the electricity we receive to run our data centers comes from renewable energy sources like hydroelectric power, wind turbines, and solar panels. We plan to use as many renewable energy sources as possible.

In addition, since 2021, GoMining has been a member of the Bitcoin Mining Council, an organization that is engaged in finding and implementing opportunities for lower energy consumption by miners and improving the environmental friendliness of the entire industry.
