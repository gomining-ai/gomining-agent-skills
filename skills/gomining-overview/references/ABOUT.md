# GoMining Platform

GoMining makes Bitcoin mining accessible as a digital-native experience. Physical hashrate is tokenized into digital miners, so anyone can earn real BTC rewards, without owning hardware, managing energy contracts, or running infrastructure. Beyond mining, the platform offers a full financial layer: a Visa card, a multi-asset wallet, governance through the GOMINING token, and a growing ecosystem of products built on top.

## Core Products

- **Miners** — Each miner is backed by a real share of GoMining's physical hashrate. Earn daily BTC proportional to your computing power and energy efficiency, with no hardware to operate.
- **GOMINING Token** — The ecosystem's utility and governance token. Stake to earn protocol rewards, lock as veGOMINING to vote on proposals, and benefit from deflationary burn-and-mint mechanics.
- **GoMining Card** — A virtual Visa debit card that converts your chosen crypto asset into fiat at the moment of each transaction. Works with Apple Pay and Google Wallet. Earn TH cashback on every purchase.
- **Miner Wars** — A free competitive game built on real Bitcoin blockchain data. Join a clan to battle for BTC prizes.
- **Wallet** — A secure multi-asset wallet for holding BTC, GOMINING, USDT, and USDC. Includes Simple Earn, a yield-generating option for idle assets held on the platform.
- **Promos** — Referral bonuses, GoBoxes, VIP program, raffles, and a bounty initiative, many ways to earn additional rewards within the ecosystem.
- **Academy** — Educational resources covering Bitcoin mining fundamentals, miner mechanics, tokenomics, and DeFi strategies, structured for both new and experienced GoMining users.
- **Travel** — Book hotels worldwide using your GoMining wallet balance. Earn TH cashback on every stay, automatically credited to your miner.
- **Launchpad** — An incubation platform for new projects launching within the GoMining ecosystem. Token holders get early access and exclusive allocations before public availability.
