# Use Cases

GoMining combines Bitcoin mining, DeFi, and everyday crypto spending into a single platform. Here are the main ways people use it.

## Earn Passive Bitcoin Income

The most direct use case: own digital miners, earn daily BTC, without buying physical hardware, managing electricity contracts, or running infrastructure yourself.

Each miner earns a share of Bitcoin proportional to its **computing power (TH/s)** relative to the total pool hashrate. Energy costs are automatically deducted from gross rewards, so what arrives in your wallet is already net income.

**Who it's for:** Anyone who wants BTC exposure tied to real mining economics rather than price speculation alone.

**Key levers:**
- Buy miners with higher TH/s to increase gross BTC per day
- Buy miners with lower W/TH to reduce energy deductions and increase net yield
- Use a **Miner Subscription** to automatically grow your hashrate month-over-month without manual purchases

## Spend Crypto Like Cash

GoMining Cards let you use your crypto holdings for everyday purchases, without manually converting to fiat first.

When you pay, your chosen asset (BTC, USDT, USDC, or GOMINING) is converted to the transaction currency in real time. The card works anywhere Visa is accepted, and integrates with Apple Pay and Google Wallet for contactless payments.

**Who it's for:** Crypto holders who want to use their assets day-to-day rather than keeping them idle.

**How it compounds:** Every card transaction earns **TH cashback** credited directly to a miner of your choice, so spending crypto also grows your mining power.

A **Gift Miner** is automatically issued when you create your first GoMining Card, giving you immediate mining income without an additional purchase.

## Participate in Ecosystem Governance

GOMINING token holders can lock their tokens to receive **veGOMINING**, vote-escrow tokens that grant governance rights over the protocol.

The longer the lock period, the more voting power received. Governance decisions include reward distribution parameters, protocol upgrades, and new product launches.

**Who it's for:** Long-term holders who want a say in how the platform evolves, and who are willing to commit their tokens for a defined period in exchange for that influence.

**Additional upside:** Staking GOMINING also earns a share of protocol revenue distributed to stakers, creating a yield layer on top of governance rights.

veGOMINING is non-transferable. Voting power decays as the lock expiration approaches, incentivising longer-term commitment.

## Generate Yield on Idle Assets

**Simple Earn** in the GoMining wallet lets you put unused crypto to work. Rather than holding assets that sit idle between mining payouts, you can opt into yield generation directly inside the platform.

**Who it's for:** Users who accumulate BTC or stablecoin rewards from mining and want to maximise return on those holdings while they decide what to do with them.

## Scale with Subscriptions

Instead of manually buying miners each month, GoMining offers subscriptions that grow your mining power on autopilot.

A **Miner Subscription** adds a fixed amount of TH/s to your miner every month. You set it once and your hashrate compounds over time without any additional action. Subscriptions can be paused, adjusted, or cancelled at any time.

For users looking for broader platform benefits, a **VIP Membership** unlocks higher maintenance discounts, priority support, and exclusive perks based on your activity level.

**Who it's for:** Users who want consistent hashrate growth without manually monitoring the marketplace every month.

## Win in Miner Wars

Beyond passive income, GoMining has a competitive layer: **Miner Wars**, weekly events where miners compete for bonus BTC and GOMINING prize pools.

Performance in Miner Wars depends on your miner's stats, the **Spells** you activate, and the strength of the **Clan** you join. Top performers and clans earn rewards above and beyond standard daily mining income.

**Who it's for:** Active users who want to maximise total return through engagement, not just passive holding.

## Get Early Access via Launchpad

GoMining Launchpad gives GOMINING token holders priority access to new projects launching within the ecosystem, before they become publicly available.

**Who it's for:** Token holders who want to benefit from ecosystem growth beyond the core platform, early-stage access with allocations sized to their holdings.
