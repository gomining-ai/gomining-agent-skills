# Referral & Ambassador Program

### What is the GoMining Referral Program?

The GoMining Referral Program lets you earn rewards by inviting new users to the platform. You receive rewards for referral activity, such as miner purchases, upgrades, or the use of supported features.

Rewards are credited in **GOMINING** (or **USDT**) to your internal wallet. They can be used within the platform or withdrawn to an external wallet.

### Who can participate in the referral program?

Any registered GoMining user can participate — just share your unique referral link or code to invite others.

### How can I invite friends to GoMining?

To invite friends to GoMining, share your referral link or referral code from the Referral Program page.

Also, you can make your invitation stand out by customizing it before you share it. In the referral section, choose a banner, background, or sharing format to personalize your message.

For best results, ask your friends to enter your referral code:

* as a promo code during their first purchase, or
* on their profile page after registration.

> A user will not count as your referral if they:
> * Created their account before you
> * Registered via advertising or promo campaign
> * Created their account more than 30 days ago
> * Already made GoMining purchases
> * Were previously linked to another referrer

### What rewards do my friends and I receive?

#### For your friends (referrals)

* +5% mining power on their first miner purchase if they use your code within 30 days of registering
* Cashback reward if they reach a specific card spending volume within the first 90 days
* 1 month of VIP Platinum+ subscription for free. The bonus is valid for activation only within 30 days of your referral's registration.

#### For you (referrer)

* 5% of the amount your referrals spend on miner purchases (excluding secondary market purchases)
* 5% of what your referrals spend on miner upgrades (such as power or energy efficiency)
* 2.5% of tokens your referrals spend on Miner Wars boosts (calculated weekly)
* Extra rewards once your referral reaches a specific card spending volume within 90 days
* 10% of your referrals' rewards in Simple Earn, credited once per day

> Reward conditions are subject to change. For the latest terms and requirements, please visit your Referral Program page.

#### New products & rewards

As GoMining adds new products and services, more referral rewards may become available. Always check the Referral Program for the latest supported products.

> Only purchases made directly within the GoMining platform count toward referral rewards. Purchases outside the platform (OTC), through managers, or via other channels may not qualify.

### What is the GoMining Ambassador Program?

The Ambassador Program is for users who are highly active in:

* consistently referring new users, or
* promoting GoMining on social media, online communities, or public channels.

Ambassadors receive more opportunities and benefits than regular referral participants, such as:

* extra rewards for electricity payments made by their referrals
* access to exclusive activities, campaigns, and giveaways
* other special program opportunities

The more you promote and refer, the more rewards you can earn as an ambassador.

### How are electricity rewards calculated for referrals?

As an ambassador, you earn extra rewards when your referrals pay for electricity.

You only receive these rewards for referrals who register after you become an ambassador.

Reward Calculation Schedule:

* For referrals in **Mining mode**, rewards are calculated daily.
* For referrals in **Miner Wars mode**, rewards are calculated weekly after each game cycle ends.

Daily electricity rewards are calculated with this formula:

> Miner Power (TH) × Energy Efficiency (W/TH) × (24 / 1000) × 0.005

> In Miner Wars mode, Ambassador electricity rewards from a referral's miners are calculated based on the weighted average Energy Efficiency (W/TH).

Electricity rewards are paid out separately from standard referral rewards.

### How to Become a GoMining Ambassador

You may be granted ambassador status if you:

* consistently and fairly refer new users, **or**
* have a strong social or community presence, and actively promote GoMining.

Ambassador status is not automatic or guaranteed.

GoMining may also assign ambassador status on a trial basis to evaluate your activity and compliance with program rules.

### Can ambassador status be revoked?

Yes. GoMining reserves the right to review, change, or revoke ambassador status if:

* Program rules are violated.
* The user shows insufficient promotional activity.
* Referral behavior does not align with the program's goals and principles.

### What if my ambassador status is revoked?

If your ambassador status is revoked:

* You will still be part of the standard referral program.
* You keep earning regular referral rewards for eligible purchases and actions.
* However, you lose all extra ambassador benefits.

### What actions are allowed in the referral program?

You can do the following as part of the referral program:

* Share your referral link or code to invite new users.
* Offer a 5% cashback bonus on a friend's first miner purchase when they use your referral code.
* Present yourself as an official GoMining ambassador only after receiving confirmation of your status.
* Use official GoMining materials and news, but do not alter or misrepresent them.
* Promote the GoMining platform features honestly and transparently.
* Use only the approved promotional materials provided in your personal account.

> When inviting users, make sure they understand:
> * They are registering as your referral.
> * This link or code is only for the referral program. It is not a regular GoMining promotion or site-wide promo code.

### What actions are not allowed in the referral program?

You must not do any of the following:

* Create multiple accounts to get extra referral or bonus rewards.
* Transfer or resell miners or tokens between your own or related accounts for profit.
* Use misleading information, such as fake discounts, guaranteed income claims, or advertising "free" services.
* Pretend to be a GoMining employee or present your own resources as official GoMining materials.
* Promote your referral links using GoMining-branded search terms (like "GoMining" or "GoMining promo code").
* Target members of official GoMining communities to sign up with your referral link.
* Use bots, automated signups, or any type of fraud.

> GoMining may ask you to stop any referral method they consider dishonest, misleading, or against the platform's goals.

### What counts as abuse in the referral or ambassador program?

Abuse means using the referral or ambassador program primarily for your own benefit rather than bringing in truly new, independent users.

Examples of abuse:

* Creating multiple accounts for yourself.
* Using accounts of family members or people in your household just to get referral rewards.
* Transferring assets between your own or related accounts to collect referral rewards.

### What happens if you seriously violate the rules?

If GoMining confirms abuse or a serious rule violation, they may take the following actions:

* Remove your ambassador status.
* Block you from participating in the referral program.
* Suspend payouts or apply other penalties as outlined in the platform's rules.
