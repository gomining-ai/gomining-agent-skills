# Promos Overview

GoMining offers a variety of promotional programs to help you earn more, reduce costs, and get rewarded for your activity on the platform: whether you're a new user, an active participant, or someone who grows the community.

**Ways to earn through promos:**

* Earn BTC rewards with a free starter miner while completing onboarding tasks
* Get rewarded in GOMINING tokens by completing weekly Bounty tasks
* Earn referral rewards by inviting friends, and grow further as an Ambassador
* Unlock VIP-tier benefits and exclusive GoBoxes as your activity increases
* Win miners, tokens, and other prizes through Raffles and Contests

### In this section

#### Bonus Miner

A free miner is given to every new user to experience mining from day one.

#### Referral & Ambassador Program

Earn rewards by bringing new users to the platform.

#### Bounty Program

Earn GOMINING tokens by completing tasks in weekly cycles.

#### VIP Program

A tiered system that rewards your activity and investment on the platform.

#### GoBoxes

Mystery boxes are earned by reaching new VIP levels for the first time.

#### Raffles & Contests

Periodic prize campaigns that reward your regular in-app activity.
