# Wallet Overview

The GoMining wallet is your built-in account for managing crypto assets on the platform. Assets are stored off-chain, meaning they are not tied to any specific blockchain after deposit. This lets you, for example, deposit on one network and withdraw on another.

**With your wallet, you can:**

* Store and manage BTC, GOMINING, USDT, USDC, BNB, ETH, SOL, TON, and more
* Buy digital miners and pay for in-app purchases
* Receive BTC mining rewards and GOMINING token rewards
* Earn passive BTC rewards on your idle balance with Simple Earn
* Participate in governance using veGOMINING votes
