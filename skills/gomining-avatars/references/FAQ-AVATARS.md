# GoMiners Avatars

## What are GoMiners Avatars?

GoMiners are limited-edition digital avatars that unlock exclusive benefits in the GoMining ecosystem. Think of them as superheroes with special abilities: they improve your BTC earnings and open access to perks you wouldn't get otherwise.

There will be 10 series in total, each featuring around 100 unique avatars.

---

## What benefits do I get from owning an avatar?

Every avatar gives its owner several benefits:

* Access to **GoClub** with private meetups, member-only content, and more (Coming soon)
* A **5% discount** on miner creation and power upgrades
* Early **access to presales** for new miner collections
* A **Welcome pack** with official GoMining merch (for your first claimed avatar)

In addition to these core benefits, each avatar series has its own **unique ability**. For example, Sentinels give a 20% discount on spells in Miner Wars, while Cryptiles improve your mining farm's efficiency by 1 W/TH (but not below 20 W/TH).

---

## How can I get an avatar?

You have two options:

**Claim for free with a key**

Keys can be found inside GoBoxes — mystery boxes earned via VIP program, raffles, and other promotions. When you win a key, it appears automatically in your profile.

To use the key, go to the Marketplace → GoMiners, pick an avatar, and claim it. You can store it in your GoMining wallet or any Ethereum wallet linked to your account.

Once claimed, the avatar is permanently yours — you can keep it or sell it later.

**Buy on the Marketplace**

You can also purchase avatars directly from other users on the Marketplace. All benefits will still be available to you as the new owner.

The Welcome pack is only awarded once — if the previous owner claimed it, you won't be able to claim it again.

---

## How do I use my avatar?

As soon as you claim an avatar, most of its core perks activate automatically, but two things require extra action:

* **Unique ability** — To enable it, set the avatar as your profile picture in your profile.
* **Welcome Pack** — It isn't shipped automatically. You'll see a button on the avatar's page to order it manually.

---

## Can I own multiple avatars?

Yes — but their **benefits don't stack**. For example, you won't get double discounts or can't use two unique abilities at the same time. Instead, you can switch which ability is active by changing your profile picture.

This way, you can collect avatars from different series and switch abilities depending on your current needs.

---

## Can I sell my avatar?

Yes. Tap **Sell** on the avatar card to list it on the Marketplace. While it's listed, you still keep all benefits until someone else buys it.
