# About

### What is the GOMINING token?

GOMINING is the utility token of the GoMining ecosystem, used for payments, rewards, governance, and access to platform features.

---

### How can the token be used?

The GOMINING token gives you several ways to benefit from the ecosystem:

* Purchase miners, avatars, and merch on our Marketplace
* Pay for miner maintenance inside with up to 20% discount
* Receive royalties and rewards
* Activate boosts in the Miner Wars game, and more
