# GOMINING Token

The GOMINING token is the utility token of the GoMining ecosystem. It powers everyday activity on the platform from paying maintenance fees and buying miners to participating in governance and earning weekly rewards.

**With the GOMINING token, you can:**

* Pay miner maintenance fees with up to a 20% discount
* Buy miners, avatars, and merch on the GoMining Marketplace
* Lock tokens to earn veGOMINING votes and receive weekly GOMINING rewards
* Participate in governance voting to influence how ecosystem rewards are allocated
* Activate boosts in Miner Wars and contribute to the ecosystem's deflationary model

The GOMINING tokenomics is a deflationary system built on four interconnected mechanics: Epochs, Burn & Mint cycles, veGOMINING staking, and governance voting.
