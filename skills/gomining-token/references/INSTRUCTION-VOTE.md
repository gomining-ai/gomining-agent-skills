# Vote Using veGOMINING Votes

You can vote using veGOMINING votes either from your virtual wallet or your Ethereum wallet.

1. Go to the **My lock** section and make sure you have veGOMINING votes in your GoMining or Ethereum wallet
2. Head to the **Voting** section and connect the wallet that holds your votes
3. Select the active voting you want to join, choose your preferred option, and click **Vote**

#### Key things to know

Voting does not spend your votes; it simply confirms ownership.

Voting rules differ depending on the wallet type:

* **GoMining wallet**: When voting, you always use all votes from all locks combined in your GoMining wallet. You can't split votes across different options — all votes go to one choice.
* **Ethereum wallet**: When voting, you use only the votes from that specific wallet. If you have votes on several Ethereum wallets, you can connect them one by one and vote separately from each.

Voting with an Ethereum wallet is not available in the iOS app — please use the web version. Voting with a virtual wallet is supported on all platforms, including iOS.

Your vote stays active as long as you have veGOMINING in your wallet and don't revoke it. If you want to change your choice, simply revoke and vote again.
