## $10 Investment Tier

### Available Options

| Option | Min | Requirements | What You Get |
|--------|-----|-------------|--------------|
| Simple Earn | $10 | KYC Level-1, non-restricted region | Daily BTC yield on USDT/USDC/BTC balance |
| Buy GOMINING tokens | Any | None | Tokens for future use, maintenance discounts |

### Recommended: Simple Earn

1. Complete KYC Level-1 verification
2. Deposit $10 in USDT or USDC
3. Activate Simple Earn (one button)
4. Receive daily BTC dividends at 07:00 UTC

**Expected outcome:** ~9.85% APR on stablecoins, paid daily in BTC. On $10, that's roughly $0.98/year in BTC.

**Alternative — Token accumulation:** Buy $10 of GOMINING tokens. Hold for future miner purchases or use for 20% maintenance discount when you upgrade to a miner later.

### Limitations

| What You Can't Do | Why |
|-------------------|-----|
| Buy a Digital Miner | Entry miners start ~$50+ |
| Get GoMining Card | $100 minimum balance |
| Reach Silver VIP | Need 10 TH or 100 veGOMINING |

> **Region restriction:** Simple Earn is not available in USA, EEA, UK, and sanctioned countries.

## Risk Considerations

| Risk | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|--------|------------|
| **BTC price volatility** | Mining rewards fluctuate in USD value | Long-term horizon; rewards are in BTC |
| **Mining difficulty increase** | Per-TH BTC rewards decrease over time | Offset by potential BTC price appreciation |
| **Maintenance costs** | Daily electricity fees reduce net profit | Stack discounts: GMT (20%) + VIP + Service Button (3%) |
| **GOMINING token volatility** | Token value may decrease | Use tokens functionally (maintenance, governance) |
| **Simple Earn APR variability** | Rates are not guaranteed, can be zero | Diversify income streams; don't rely solely on earn |
| **Regional restrictions** | Simple Earn blocked in USA, EEA, UK | Use miners + card instead in restricted regions |
| **MNPL commitment** | Monthly payments required | Budget installments from mining rewards |
| **Smart contract risk** | NFTs are on-chain assets | Use reputable wallets; secure private keys |
