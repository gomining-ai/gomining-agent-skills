## Maintenance Cost Reduction

Maintenance is the biggest drag on mining profitability. Stack these discounts:

| Discount Source | Max Savings | How to Activate |
|-----------------|------------|-----------------|
| **GOMINING token payment** | Up to 20% | Hold 360+ days of maintenance in GOMINING tokens |
| **VIP level discount** | Up to 6% (Elite) | Increase hashrate or veGOMINING |
| **Service Button** | 3% | Click daily for 10 consecutive days |
| **Mining Mode** | Variable | Based on Burn & Mint cycle |

### GOMINING Token Maintenance Discount Tiers

The discount scales with how many days of maintenance you hold in GOMINING tokens:

| Token Balance (days of maintenance) | Discount |
|-------------------------------------|----------|
| 30 days | 5% |
| 90 days | 10% |
| 180 days | 15% |
| 360 days | 20% |

**Tip:** Buy GOMINING tokens equal to ~360 days of your total maintenance cost to lock in the maximum 20% discount. At a miner with $1/day maintenance, that's ~$360 in tokens.

### Service Button Strategy

The Service Button provides 3% maintenance discount when clicked daily for 10 consecutive days. Missing a day resets the counter.

**Tip:** Set a daily reminder. The 3% compounds with other discounts.

## Reinvestment Compounding

Mining rewards can be reinvested into additional TH/s, creating a compounding effect.

### How Reinvestment Works

1. Miner generates daily BTC rewards
2. Reinvest BTC → converts to TH/s added to your miner
3. More TH/s = more daily BTC = more to reinvest
4. Silver I+ users get a **+5% reinvestment bonus** (more TH per BTC reinvested)

### Compounding Impact Example

Starting with 50 TH/s and reinvesting all mining rewards:

| Timeline | Without Reinvest | With Reinvest | With Reinvest + 5% Bonus |
|----------|-----------------|---------------|--------------------------|
| Month 1 | 50 TH | ~51 TH | ~51.5 TH |
| Month 6 | 50 TH | ~56 TH | ~58 TH |
| Year 1 | 50 TH | ~63 TH | ~67 TH |

> These are illustrative estimates. Actual compounding depends on BTC price, mining difficulty changes, and maintenance costs.

### When to Reinvest vs Withdraw

| Scenario | Recommendation |
|----------|---------------|
| Building toward a VIP tier | Reinvest — faster hashrate growth |
| Silver I+ and growing | Reinvest — benefit from +5% bonus |
| Near a VIP threshold (e.g., 48/50 TH for Gold I) | Reinvest aggressively to hit tier |
| At target VIP tier, stable | Hybrid — reinvest 50%, earn/withdraw 50% |
| Need liquidity | Withdraw to wallet or deposit in Simple Earn |

## VIP Progression Optimization

### Cheapest Path to Each Tier

There are two ways to advance VIP: hashrate (TH) or veGOMINING. Often, locking tokens is cheaper.

| Target | Via TH (buy miners) | Via veGOMINING (lock tokens) | Cheaper Path |
|--------|---------------------|------------------------------|-------------|
| Silver I | 10 TH (~$250–500) | 100 VE (~$30–60 in GMT) | Tokens |
| Gold I | 50 TH (~$1,500–3,000) | 500 VE (~$150–300 in GMT) | Tokens |
| Platinum I | 200 TH (~$6,000–12,000) | 2,000 VE (~$600–1,200 in GMT) | Tokens |
| Diamond I | 500 TH (~$15,000–30,000) | 5,000 VE (~$1,500–3,000 in GMT) | Tokens |

> veGOMINING costs depend on lock duration — longer locks yield more VE per token. Prices based on GMT ~$0.30.

**Key insight:** Locking tokens gives you VIP benefits AND staking rewards (20% of weekly Burn & Mint distribution). The hashrate path gives mining income but costs 5–10x more for the same VIP tier.

**Optimal strategy:** Combine both — buy miners for BTC income, lock tokens for VIP tier + governance rewards.

### Platinum+ Subscription as Shortcut

For users whose natural VIP is below Platinum I:

| Benefit | Platinum+ Value | Break-Even Point |

| 2% card cashback | Extra 1.5% vs Bronze | ~$3,267/month card spend |
| x1.2 Earn multiplier | Extra 1.97% APR on stables | ~$30K in Simple Earn |
| 2.1% maintenance discount | Reduces daily costs | High-TH miners |
| 15% spell discounts | Miner Wars savings | Active players |

**Worth it when:** You have substantial earn balances ($5K+) OR actively use the Card ($2K+/month spend) OR play Miner Wars competitively.

## Timing Considerations

### When to Buy Miners

- **BTC price dip:** TH/s price may lag BTC drops, but mining rewards in BTC stay constant — you get more mining at similar cost
- **After difficulty adjustment downward:** More BTC per TH in the next ~2 weeks
- **New collection drops:** Primary marketplace sometimes offers better pricing than secondary

### When to Lock Tokens

- **Before Burn & Mint epoch:** Lock tokens before the weekly cycle (Tuesday) to participate in that week's distribution
- **Long lock preferred:** Longer lock periods give more veGOMINING per token, meaning cheaper VIP progression

### When to Activate Simple Earn

- **Before 23:59 UTC:** Balances must be in Simple Earn by snapshot time to earn that day
- **Immediately after depositing:** Don't leave crypto idle — activate Simple Earn on any balance you're not using

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

| Mistake | Why It Hurts | What to Do Instead |
|---------|-------------|-------------------|
| Paying maintenance in BTC | Missing 20% GOMINING discount | Always pay with GOMINING tokens |
| Idle wallet balances | Zero yield on unused funds | Activate Simple Earn on all idle crypto |
| Ignoring Service Button | Losing 3% maintenance discount | Set daily reminder, click 10 consecutive days |
| Buying TH for VIP when tokens are cheaper | Overpaying 5–10x for same VIP tier | Lock tokens for veGOMINING instead |
| Not reinvesting at Silver I+ | Missing +5% compounding bonus | Reinvest mining rewards for bonus TH |
| Single income stream | Concentration risk | Diversify: mining + earn + cashback + staking |
| Over-leveraging with MNPL | Installment stress if BTC drops | Keep 50%+ of portfolio in direct purchases |
| Depositing after 23:59 UTC snapshot | Missed earn cycle for that day | Deposit before 23:59 UTC |
