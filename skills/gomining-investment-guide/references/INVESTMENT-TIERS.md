## BTC Loans

Available to users with BTC in their wallet — borrow stablecoins without selling Bitcoin.

| Parameter | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Collateral | BTC |
| Borrow currencies | USDT, USDC |
| Use case | Liquidity without selling BTC |
| Auto-renewal | Optional |

**Investment use cases:**
- Borrow against BTC holdings to buy miners without selling BTC
- Use borrowed stablecoins in Simple Earn while keeping BTC exposure
- Fund MNPL down payments from BTC collateral

> Liquidation risk: if BTC price drops significantly, collateral may be liquidated. Monitor LTV ratio.
