# Rarity & Achievements

## Miner Uniqueness

The Miner Uniqueness Leaderboard is a ranking system in GoMining that measures the uniqueness of miners. It is based on how rare the miner's traits are within its collection and the achievements it has earned.

### How is miner uniqueness calculated?

The uniqueness of a miner is determined as the average of two factors:

* **Rarity Rating** — the rarity of the miner's traits within its collection
* **Achievement Rating** — the number of achievements earned through mining, upgrades, and victories in the MinerWars game

Uniqueness Rating = (Rarity Rating + Achievement Rating) / 2

Miner uniqueness can increase or decrease since it is relative to other miners.

### How does a miner enter the Miner Uniqueness Leaderboard?

All new miners automatically enter the leaderboard the day after purchase.

### How can I improve my miner's position in the leaderboard?

Once purchased, a miner's rarity cannot be changed. However, the owner can improve the miner's ranking by earning new achievements.

### Does the leaderboard position affect the miner's resale price?

The leaderboard position does not directly affect the price of a miner.

### How often is the leaderboard updated?

The leaderboard is updated daily.

## Miner Rarity

Miner rarity indicates how rare its components are within a specific collection.

### How is the miner's Rarity Rating calculated?

Rarity Rating = (Number of miners with Rarity Score ≤ this miner's Rarity Score / Total number of miners in the collection) × 100

### How is a miner's Rarity Score determined?

A miner's Rarity Score is the sum of the Rarity Scores of all its traits.

Rarity Score of a trait = 1 / (% rarity of trait)

For example, if a specific trait has a rarity of 2.5%, its Rarity Score would be: 1 / 2.5% = 40

## Miner Achievements

Achievements are rewards given to miners for their active participation in the GoMining system.

### What types of achievements exist, and how are they earned?

Achievements are divided into several categories:

* For mining duration (earned at 100, 500, or 1000 days of mining)
* For the amount of Bitcoin mined by the miner (earned at 0.1, 0.5, or 1 BTC)
* For reinvesting mined Bitcoin into GOMINING tokens (earned after reinvesting 100, 500, or 1000 tokens)
* For mining blocks in the Odyssey league in Miner Wars
* Special achievements — awarded during limited-time events, promotions, or holidays

### Can achievements be transferred?

No, achievements are tied to a specific miner and remain with it permanently, even if it is resold on the marketplace.
