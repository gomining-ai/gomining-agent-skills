# Subscriptions

GoMining offers two types of subscriptions that give you ongoing benefits for a recurring payment — no one-time purchases required.

**With a GoMining subscription, you can:**

* Automatically grow your miner's power every month without manual upgrades
* Access Platinum I VIP privileges instantly, regardless of your current VIP level

---

### In this section

#### Miner Subscription

A monthly plan that automatically increases your miner's computing power. The longer you stay subscribed, the higher the bonus TH you receive with each payment — up to +8%.

#### Platinum+ Subscription

A monthly subscription that gives you access to Platinum I VIP privileges without changing your VIP level or requiring you to hold TH or tokens.
