# Miners

## Miners

Digital miners are backed by real computing power in GoMining's physical data centers. When you own one, you hold a share of that hardware and earn daily BTC rewards proportional to your miner's power and energy efficiency.

**With your digital miners, you can:**

* Earn daily BTC rewards in Mining mode
* Compete for weekly BTC and GOMINING prizes in Miner Wars
* Upgrade computing power (TH) and energy efficiency (W/TH) to maximize net rewards
* Grow your miner's power automatically month-by-month with a subscription
* Sell or trade miners on GoMining's marketplace or any public marketplace
