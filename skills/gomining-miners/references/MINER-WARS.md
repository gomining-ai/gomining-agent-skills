# Miner Wars

### What are Miner Wars?

Miner Wars is a free, competitive blockchain-based game that simulates Bitcoin mining through clan battles and personal competitions. Players join clans to compete for BTC rewards and earn GOMINING tokens in personal battles. The game uses real Bitcoin blockchain data to ensure fair and transparent outcomes.

**Players can earn 2 types of rewards:**

* BTC in clan battles
* GOMINING tokens in personal competitions

Any user can participate for free using a bonus miner — no need to own a digital miner.

---

### Game Rules

Key prohibited activities:

#### Game system abuse

The following actions are strictly prohibited in Miner Wars and may result in temporary suspension or permanent account ban:

* Using any unauthorized third-party software or bots
* Operating multiple accounts
* Colluding with other players or engaging in any coordinated activity intended to exploit the game, manipulate outcomes, or harm the company, other players, or clans
* League Hopping or Clan Cycling (the practice of intentionally moving between multiple clans or accounts to manipulate league placement, bypass difficulty scaling, or monopolize promotion slots)
