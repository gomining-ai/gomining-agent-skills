# How to Sell Your Miner

## Before You Sell

Complete these steps for the miner you want to sell:

1. **Turn off TH reinvestment** — if this miner is the reinvestment target, disable it or assign another miner.
2. **Cancel active subscriptions** — cancel any active power upgrade subscriptions on this miner.
3. **Mint the miner (if needed)** — if your miner was created as a Minebox and is stored in your GoMining Wallet without being minted, you will see a **Mint** button instead of **Sell**. Minting assigns the miner to a blockchain network and makes it tradeable.

### How to Mint

1. Set up a crypto wallet (MetaMask or any WalletConnect-compatible wallet). Save your seed phrase.
2. Add crypto to cover network fees:
   - ~$2 in BNB for BSC
   - ~$10 in ETH for Ethereum
3. Select your preferred network, connect your wallet, confirm the minting transaction.

Once minted, the miner appears in your wallet on the chosen network while continuing to earn rewards in your GoMining account.

---

## 90-Day Selling Restriction

Miners purchased or upgraded via **Apple Pay, Google Pay, or Ecommpay** cannot be sold or withdrawn for **90 days** from the date of the most recent qualifying purchase or upgrade.

| Scenario | 90-day applies? |
|----------|----------------|
| Purchased via Apple/Google/Ecommpay | Yes |
| Purchased via crypto | No |
| Upgraded via Apple/Google/Ecommpay | Yes — timer resets |
| MNPL — final installment paid | Yes — 90 days from final payment |

- All timing is based on UTC.
- See GoMining Terms of Service, Section 2.2 for full details.

## How to Sell

### Step 1 — Find the Sell button

The **Mint** and **Sell** buttons are in the menu behind the three dots next to the Upgrade button on the miner card. The **Sell** button only appears if the miner is eligible for selling.

### Step 2 — Set your price

All Marketplace payments use **GOMINING tokens**. You can:
- Set a fixed price manually.
- Link your price to the market average for miners with similar power and efficiency.

**Marketplace fee:** 5% is added on top of your price and paid by the buyer.

> Double-check your price before confirming — once listed, the miner can be purchased instantly and the sale cannot be undone.

### Step 3 — Confirm the listing

**GoMining Wallet miners:** Tap Sell — the miner appears on the Marketplace immediately.

**BSC / Ethereum / TON / Solana miners:**
1. Connect the wallet on the same network as the miner.
2. Approve the smart contract (on BSC/ETH: permission for transfer; on TON/SOL: miner moves temporarily to the Marketplace wallet).
3. Ensure your wallet has enough BNB / ETH / TON / SOL for network fees.
4. Tap Sell.

---

## While Your Miner is Listed

- Keeps earning rewards (Mining Mode or Miner Wars) until sold.
- Cannot be upgraded, withdrawn, set for TH reinvestment, or have subscriptions added.
- To change the price: remove from sale, then relist at the new price.

---

## Secondary Market Miners

Miners purchased on the secondary market **do not count toward VIP progression** at time of purchase. However, any **upgrades** made to those miners via GoMining do count toward VIP.
