# Upgrade Rewards and Mode Switching

## Reward Processing After Upgrades

Upgraded rewards do not apply immediately. The timeline depends on the mode:

### Mining Mode (daily cycle)

- Mining days run from **00:00 UTC to 00:00 UTC**.
- Upgrades purchased during a day start earning from the **next full daily cycle**.
- Example: upgrade purchased Monday at 18:00 UTC → upgraded capacity earns from Tuesday 00:00 UTC → credited Wednesday.

### Miner Wars Mode (weekly cycle)

- Rewards are calculated at **12:00 UTC every Tuesday** based on blocks mined by the clan.
- If you switch to Miner Wars mode during a week, daily BTC rewards stop for those days. Miner Wars rewards for that week are still paid out.

---

## Switching Between Modes

| Switch | Effect |
|--------|--------|
| Mining → Miner Wars | No BTC rewards on the day of switching, even for one round of Miner Wars. Miner Wars rewards based on clan blocks. |
| Miner Wars → Mining | Daily BTC rewards resume the **following day**. |

---

## When Did I Join a Clan Mid-Week?

You do not need to complete a full weekly cycle. If your clan wins at least one block, you receive your Miner Wars reward on the next **Tuesday** at the end of the current cycle.

---

## Common Issues

**Why are my upgraded rewards delayed?**
Transactions may take a short time to process. After purchasing an upgrade, allow some time before expecting the change to reflect in your account.

**Why did my rewards stop?**
Possible reasons: you switched to Miner Wars mode, or your clan did not mine any blocks during the applicable period. Check your mode settings and clan activity.
