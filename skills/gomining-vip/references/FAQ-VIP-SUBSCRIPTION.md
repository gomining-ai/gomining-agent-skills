# Platinum+ Subscription

### What is the Platinum+ Subscription?

Platinum+ Subscription gives you access to Platinum I VIP privileges, no matter what your current VIP level is.

It's the fastest way to unlock top-tier benefits without having to upgrade your mining power or hold large amounts of tokens.

---

### Does the Subscription Change my VIP Level?

No.

The Platinum+ Subscription does not change your VIP level. Your VIP status will remain the same before, during, and after your subscription.

---

### What benefits does the subscription provide?

With an active Platinum+ subscription, you get:

* Access to **Platinum I** privileges
* A **15% discount** on boosts purchased in Miner Wars

The 15% subscription discount on boosts does not stack with GoMiners Avatar discounts. The highest discount will apply by default.

However, the Platinum+ subscription does not offer GoBoxes as rewards for reaching VIP levels. You can earn GoBoxes only by advancing through the VIP levels on your own.

---

### Who can get a Platinum+ Subscription?

Any user can activate a Platinum+ Subscription, regardless of their current VIP level.

If your VIP level is already higher than Platinum I, activating a Platinum+ Subscription will not take away or change your current privileges.

---

### What is the subscription cost, and how can I pay?

- Subscription price: $29.99 per 30 days
- Each billing period lasts 30 days.

You can pay for the subscription using:

- A bank card (processed through Ecommpay)
- GOMINING tokens from your account balance

---

### How long does the subscription last, and what happens when it ends or is canceled?

Subscriptions renew every 30 days. If a payment fails, your Platinum+ subscription will end, and all benefits will be removed.

If you cancel your subscription manually, you will keep your Platinum+ benefits until the end of your current billing period.

You can reactivate Platinum+ at any time by making a new payment.

---

### Can I try the VIP Subscription for free?

Yes! Sometimes, we offer a VIP subscription for a limited time during special promotions or as a thank-you to active members.
