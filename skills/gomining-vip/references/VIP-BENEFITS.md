## Reinvestment Bonus Details

- **Activation:** Available from Silver I and above
- **Bonus:** +5% added to reinvestment amounts
- **Application:** Applied automatically when reinvesting mining rewards

## Clan Creation (Miner Wars)

- **Requirement:** Gold I VIP tier or above
- **Royalties:** 5% of GOMINING generated through Clan members' activity
- **Management:** Clan leaders can invite members, set rules, and manage Clan strategy
- **Restriction:** Clan creation is disabled if tier drops below Gold I

## Dedicated VIP Manager

- **Availability:** Platinum I and above
- **Services:** Priority support, personalized account guidance, and direct communication channel
- **Response Time:** Faster than standard support queues
