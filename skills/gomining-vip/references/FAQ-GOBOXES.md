# GoBoxes

### What is a GoBox?

A GoBox is a digital mystery box with prizes you can earn in the GoMining ecosystem — most often by reaching a new VIP level. You can preview the prize list and drop chances before opening. The prize is determined fairly using Bitcoin blockchain data.

### How do I get a GoBox?

Mainly through the VIP Program — you receive GoBoxes each time you reach a new VIP level for the first time. If you skip several levels, you get GoBoxes for each. Boxes stay in your account even if your VIP level drops, but regaining a lost level doesn't give you more boxes.

Other ways to receive a GoBox include miner creation, miner upgrades, or participation in raffles.

### What are GoBox Collections?

GoBoxes are grouped into collections, and the rewards inside depend on them.

Collections (e.g. Bronze, Silver, Gold) are linked to VIP program levels. The higher your level, the more exclusive the collection and the more valuable the prizes.

Some collections are limited. GoBoxes from limited collections are available only while the collection is active.

### What prizes can I win?

Depending on the collection — promo codes, bonus miner days, miners, GOMINING tokens, GoMiners keys, and more.

### How long do I have to open a GoBox?

7 days from receiving it. After that, any unopened GoBox becomes inactive and can't be opened.

### How is the prize chosen?

All prizes are placed into numbered cells with known ranges. When you open a box, the system calculates a winning cell number using Bitcoin blockchain data, your box's unique number, and the winning number from the previous box.

### Where can I find my GoBoxes?

In **Promos → GoBoxes** in your account, or in the VIP Program and Promo widgets on your Home screen.

### Can I receive a GoBox for VIP levels I reached before the GoBox feature was launched?

GoBoxes launched on **August 15, 2025 (19:00 UTC)** as a new incentive program.

This means you'll receive your first GoBox when you reach a new VIP level — one higher than the level you had when the feature launched.
