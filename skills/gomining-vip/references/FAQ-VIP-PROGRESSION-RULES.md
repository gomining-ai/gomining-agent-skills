# VIP Progression Rules

## What counts toward VIP level?

VIP level is determined by **permanent TH mining power** from eligible sources.

| Action | Counts toward VIP? |
|--------|-------------------|
| New miner created via "Create Miner" flow | Yes |
| Miner upgrade (any miner, via GoMining) | Yes |
| Farm power purchased via any method before August 15, 2025 | Yes |
| Secondary market miner purchase (after August 15, 2025) | No |
| Temporary/promotional mining power | No |
| Miners in unlinked external wallets | No |

## Secondary Market Miners

- Purchasing a miner from the secondary market does **not** trigger a VIP level change.
- However, **upgrading** a secondary market miner's power through GoMining **does** count.
- Example: buy a secondary market miner (0 TH added to VIP) → upgrade +4 TH → VIP qualifying power increases by 4 TH.

## Wallet Linkage

- Miners in wallets **linked** to your GoMining account count toward VIP.
- Miners withdrawn to GoMining wallet from linked wallets continue to contribute.
- If a miner is transferred to an **unlinked wallet**, it no longer counts toward VIP. This is **permanent** — returning the miner does not restore the lost VIP progress.

## GoBox Rewards

- GoBoxes are awarded when you reach a **new VIP level milestone**.
- Only levels achieved **after the GoBox feature was introduced** qualify — past levels do not retroactively earn GoBoxes.
- One GoBox is issued per new VIP level reached through eligible actions.

## Why Didn't My VIP Level Change?

1. Verify the power comes from miners created or upgraded directly via GoMining (not secondary market).
2. Check that miners are in linked wallets.
3. If not reflected immediately, log out and back in.

## Miner Purchases from "How Do Upgrades Affect VIP?" Perspective

- VIP changes are only triggered by **creating** or **upgrading** miners — passive ownership without a qualifying transaction does not advance VIP.
- Even if total TH exceeds a threshold through secondary market purchases alone, VIP will not advance unless a qualifying creation or upgrade action was taken.
