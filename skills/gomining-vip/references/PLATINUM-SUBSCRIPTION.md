# Platinum+ Subscription Reference

## Overview

The Platinum+ Subscription is a paid subscription service that grants users Platinum I-tier VIP benefits **without requiring them to accumulate TH hashrate or veGOMINING votes**. It provides an alternative path to premium benefits for users who prefer a subscription model over asset accumulation.

**Important:** The Platinum+ Subscription does **not** change your actual VIP status tier. It grants benefits equivalent to Platinum I independently of your tier progression.

1. **Subscription Model:** Users pay a recurring fee to activate Platinum+ benefits
2. **Instant Activation:** Benefits apply immediately upon subscription
3. **Independent of Holdings:** No TH or veGOMINING required
4. **Stackable Consideration:** Subscription benefits may interact with actual tier benefits — check current terms

## FAQ

### Does Platinum+ change my VIP tier?
No. Your VIP tier (Bronze, Silver, Gold, etc.) remains based on your TH or veGOMINING holdings. Platinum+ grants benefits independently.

### Can I use Platinum+ while already at a higher tier?
Yes. If you're already Diamond or Legend, Platinum+ benefits may not provide additional value for overlapping perks, but subscription-exclusive features like the x1.2 APR boost still apply.

### What happens when my subscription ends?
Platinum+ benefits stop immediately. Your actual VIP tier benefits (based on TH/veGOMINING) continue as normal.

### Is Platinum+ available worldwide?
Subscription availability follows GoMining's standard regional availability. Check the app for current eligibility.

### How do I subscribe?
Platinum+ subscription can be activated through the GoMining app under VIP or Subscription settings.
