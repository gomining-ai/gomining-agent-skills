# Tokenomics

GOMINING tokenomics is built on four interconnected mechanics: Epochs, Burn & Mint cycles, veGOMINING locks, and Voting. Together they create a self-regulating system designed to reduce circulating supply over time while rewarding active participants.

---

### In this section

#### Epochs

The time periods that define the mint-to-burn coefficient and GOMINING emission rate.

#### Burn & Mint Cycles

Weekly cycles in which spent GOMINING tokens are burned and new ones are minted.

#### veGOMINING & Locks

Lock GOMINING to receive veGOMINING — governance and reward multiplier tokens.

#### Voting

How veGOMINING holders participate in ecosystem governance decisions.
