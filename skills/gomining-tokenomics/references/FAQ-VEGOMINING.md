# veGOMINING & Locks

### What is veGOMINING?

veGOMINING votes are a key part of GoMining's tokenomics. They allow you to:

* Get rewards in GOMINING tokens
* Participate in voting on governance decisions
* Increase your VIP status to unlock exclusive benefits. All votes from your Ethereum wallets and GOMINING virtual wallet count toward your VIP status

---

### Where do veGOMINING rewards come from?

veGOMINING rewards come from the Burn & Mint mechanism. In every cycle, **20%** of newly minted tokens are allocated as rewards for veGOMINING holders.

---

### How are veGOMINING rewards distributed?

Rewards are distributed **every Tuesday** as part of the Burn & Mint cycle. The amount you receive depends on how many veGOMINING votes you hold compared to the total votes in the system. The more votes you have, the bigger your share of the reward pool.

All rewards are credited in GOMINING tokens and sent to your **GoMining virtual wallet**.

---

### How to get veGOMINING votes?

To get votes, you need to lock GOMINING tokens for 1 week to 4 years. The longer the lock, the more votes you receive.

**Ethereum wallet (MetaMask, WalletConnect)**

* Each Ethereum wallet can have only one active lock
* Requires ETH to cover network fees
* Rewards must be claimed manually, but you can enable auto-claim
* When voting, you use only the votes from that specific wallet

**GoMining wallet (in-app)**

* You can have multiple locks (up to 30) in your GoMining wallet
* No blockchain confirmations needed
* No network fees — instant lock and rewards
* Rewards are automatically sent to your wallet every week
* When voting, you always use all votes from all locks combined

**How to lock tokens:**

1. Go to the My lock section.
2. Choose the wallet type: Ethereum or GOMINING virtual wallet.
3. For Ethereum wallets, connect your wallet via MetaMask or WalletConnect.
4. Enter the amount of tokens to lock or the number of votes you want to receive.
5. Select the lock duration (1 week to 4 years).
6. For Ethereum wallets, approve the transaction and confirm.
7. For GOMINING virtual wallets, just confirm the lock.

Tokens can't be unlocked before the end of the lock period.

---

### What happens to my veGOMINING votes over time?

From the beginning to the end of your lock, the amount of your veGOMINING votes will decrease — this is part of the Tokenomics logic. It helps to maintain the value of votes and ensure fair competition.

---

### How to upgrade my lock and get more votes?

You can increase your votes anytime by locking more tokens or extending the lock period.

To upgrade, go to My lock and click on the "+" icon. You can upgrade your lock unlimited times.

Maximum period: 4 years from today.

---

### How to withdraw my rewards?

* If locked via an Ethereum wallet: You must claim rewards manually in the My lock section. Alternatively, enable auto-claim.
* If locked via a GOMINING virtual wallet: Every Tuesday, rewards are sent automatically to the same virtual wallet.

---

### Terms & Conditions

Key terms:

* Users receive veGOMINING votes by locking GOMINING tokens for 1 week to 4 years.
* Each wallet can have only one active lock, and locks are stored separately per wallet.
* Locked tokens cannot be withdrawn until the lock period ends.
* Virtual wallet locks have no fees; Ethereum wallet locks require ETH for fees.
* All locked tokens count towards the Token Discount on miner maintenance.
* Rewards are distributed weekly based on the number of veGOMINING votes.
* Only votes stored on Ethereum wallets can be used for governance; virtual wallet votes do not grant voting rights.
* veGOMINING votes also boost VIP status.
