# Voting

### What is veGOMINING Voting?

veGOMINING voting is a DeFi-based mechanism for deciding on key governance issues in the GoMining ecosystem. These include:

* Whether to activate the Burn & Mint mechanism each week
* How GOMINING rewards are distributed

---

### Voting on the Burn & Mint Mechanism

The result of this voting directly affects how many new GOMINING tokens are minted after the burn phase.

Formula: GOMINING_minted = (1 - V% × (1 - C)) × GOMINING_burnt

Where:
* **V%** is the percentage of votes "for" to permanently burn GOMINING tokens
* **C** is the mint coefficient that changes depending on the current epoch

If no one votes in favour of burning (**V% = 0**), the protocol mints the same number of tokens it burned — maintaining the total supply.

If veGOMINING holders vote to burn (**V% > 0**), fewer tokens are minted than burned, resulting in a net reduction in supply.

---

### Voting on GOMINING Reward Distribution

veGOMINING voters also decide how weekly GOMINING rewards are allocated between key features of the ecosystem:

* **Extra Mining mode discount** — additional discount on maintenance fees for all Mining mode users
* **Miner Wars prize fund** — extra GOMINING rewards for personal victories in the Odyssey league players
* **The Greedy Machines power increase** — TH upgrades for all miners from The Greedy Machines collection
* **Liquidity provider bonus** — weekly GOMINING rewards for liquidity providers
* **Bounty cycle rewards** — weekly prize pool for Bounty program participants
